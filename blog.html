Our History

Our History

Education is both my passion and mission. I served as an Assistant Professor in various degree colleges. After getting enough experience and observing the overall educational scenario I decided to quit my job as no positive change was possible in the system by remaining a part of it. Extensive reading and travelling made me become an independent thinker. Now the entire world was both my laboratory and my library. Being a continuous learner I kept on attending national and International training programs. Learning from the experiences of scholarly people has made my mind to solve problems. Inspired from the great minds like Dr. A.P Abdul Kalam, Swami vivekannda, Mr. Rattan Tata I was ready to face any challenges to accomplish my mission. God has been kind enough to have given me challenges and courage to face them so that I could grow. As a specialist in educational empowerment, I coach students from schools, colleges, universities, and also the faculty members. In the initial phase I was fortunate to receive an opportunity to gain firsthand experience with students from various schools and observe their educational environments as well as the challenges they encounter concerning emotional issues, peer pressure, retention problems, and other factors affecting their learning and emotional well-being, including career-related concerns. The initiative 'The Light Lets Ignite Together' has brought many national and international speakers together to empower numerous schools, colleges, and university students with valuable case studies. We commenced our projects by reviewing extensive scientific research available and then humbly invited distinguished scholars to join our board. Thus our extensive reading of available scientific research, along with our own investigations, provided us with deeper insights such as every child is unique and learns at their own pace and in different ways. After dedicating many years to focused research, we developed a practical and holistic program that emphasizes essential 21st-century skills. Furthermore, we concentrated on assisting students in realizing their true potential by offering support from a diverse group of experts, thereby enabling them to achieve their aspirations and dreams. Falling in love with the libraries, exposure to literature, participation in numerous national and international training sessions, and a commitment to lifelong learning have profoundly transformed my life. I had the privilege of working with various prestigious schools and other higher educational institutions, including S.D. Public School, BSBDS, Holy Angels, SOS, Mukat, New Delhi Public School, Enlighten Happy School, and as an expert speaker, I engaged with the students of Saint Kabir College, Jasdev Singh Sandhu, PMN College, International Divine College, SVIET, Gyan Jyoti Management and Technology, and participated as a speaker at the World Education Summit and All India Education Leaders Summit. This journey has transformed my life to such an extent that it has become my mission to serve the nation and empower and impact the lives of numerous students, teachers, parents, faculty members, and institutions. If you light the path for others your own path will be lighted.
