*Incredible Journey of a student*
_________________________
In the initial phase of my teaching journey, I had a student who struggled with reading English. The transformation from being unable to read English fluently to finishing a book in a day was quite a challenging task. We began enhancing his reading abilities by providing him with appropriate practice, guidance, and instilling the belief that consistent daily effort could lead to learning various skills. Gradually and steadily, he started to improve and increased his speed. After two years of dedicated efforts, he excelled in his English Medium School. Simultaneously, he was exposed to the subjects he was studying in commerce, which facilitated his ability to relate theoretical concepts to practical applications. His confidence grew progressively throughout the process, akin to a child's need for guidance, support, encouragement, appreciation, and validation. It was merely a beginning. Enrolled in college once more, he exposed himself to the real world, and proceeded further. He enrolled in an MBA program, and we once more ensured that he would acquire hands-on experience at national and international conferences. Before completing his Ph D, he worked as an Assistant Professor. We gained profound insights from the process he underwent in our fostering and guiding atmosphere. If we provide students with the proper resources, direction, and exposure to real-world problems and practical skills outside of the classroom, we can empower them. The science of good emotions, the learning mindset, and mental stability can be of great help.

About us

*Focus Touching Lives*
_________________________
Focus Touching Lives is a venture started in 2009 at Rajpura with the objective to bring about a positive change in educational scenarios. We wish to create a platform where learning is perceived correctly and education is delivered effectively. Over the past 13 years, Focus Touching Lives has been instrumental in spreading awareness about the same. Empowering teachers and students of different schools, colleges, and universities is our motto. Focus Touching Lives is one such organisation which strongly believes in continuous learning, improving, doing research, developing processes which would bring the best out of a student. It is providing answers as to how we can make education more interesting, relevant, practical and holistic. The Light House is an important initiative of Focus Touching Lives with the motto : Education is the best prevention Compassion and care is the best cure We have an ambitious project naming The Light -house with the sole purpose of inspiring, empowering and igniting students and teachers to work together for creating social leaders. To empower students with the right knowledge and skillsets of the 21st century we have been working closely with schools, colleges and universities. Training programs are conducted for college, university students and teachers. Our solution oriented approach, policy of reading diverse books, latest research papers, talking to experts is what makes us different. Through our Learning Enhancement Programmes we provide a complete support system to all the students, catering specially to the needs of slow learners or having learning disorders and Attention Deficit Hyper Activity Disorder (ADHD). We have special programmes for the students who are underachievers in their core subjects. So, it is all about inspiring and empowering. Creation of a stimulating environment can bring about good results and happy learners. It is in this process only that we can inspire and empower future citizens to contribute to society. We have programmes for teachers, parents and for the elderly. Enlightening senior citizens to age gracefully is our great concern. Again reading, interacting with experts and extensive research is what we bank upon. Life is a continuous process and so is learning. there is always a scope for improvement. Together we can make a difference !
